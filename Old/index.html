About us

Eklavya Coaching Academy is a modern, well-equipped training centre to nurture the young Indian minds studying in any medium at primary or secondary level.

Vision:

To be a respected educational academy in India through the power of nurturing.

Mision:

  • To improve students’ result by analysing their learning styles
  • To make them better learners and users of English
  • To develop their soft skills
  • To help them set and achieve their goals

Services

1. English Medium Coaching & Gujarati Medium Coaching.

  • We are providing Coaching for, GSEB, CBSE, and ICSE boards.
  • Special batches for Standard 5 to 12 Science and Commerce streams

2. MOEC Spoken English Centre.

  • Spoken English for all age group, ladies, gents and businessman

3. Self-Development Institute

  • Goal Setting.
  • Time Management.
  • Gruh Sabha

  • Smile

  • Feedback
  • Rewards

Mr Chetan Patel: Head, Coaching & Mentoring

Mr Chetan Patel, Head, Eklavya Coaching Academy, is a Master of Technology (M. Tech) from SardarVallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT) and Expert in Teaching. Apart from an M. Tech in Electrical, he has also obtained a PGDBA in Operations from Symbiosis, Pune. He worked with Essar Steel Ltd for 8 years. Among others, he has coached doctors, engineers and MBA students. He has also carried out activities for social causes in general and for efficiency improvement in children in particular. His fields of interest are student psychology, skill development, and goal setting. He has published a paper in a famous national journal of Central Power Research Institute.
